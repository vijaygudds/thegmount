Conference..!!

The conference room in The G Mount Valley Resort & Spa is the ideal venue for corporate conferences of up to 120 delegates. The flexible and modern conference room can be also used for multi-purpose events like private parties or any exhibition. Immediately accessible from the main reception area, the conference centre is perfect for such conferences or events.In addition to the multi-purpose conference centre the hotel also has 1 private board room, ranging in capacities from 6 to 25 delegates for a small conference or business meet. When the days work is done we offer a range of options to entertain and recharge your group.

Banquet..!!

The Banquet room facility at The G mount Valley Resort & Spa can cater upto 140 people indoor. We also offer lush green gardens which can hold a wedding or a private party upto 500 people.
