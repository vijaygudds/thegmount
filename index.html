Kumbalgarh

Kumbalgarh

Kumbhalgarh, a world heritage site is situated 84kms north west of Udaipur by road. Cradled in the Arravli ranges, is the most importan fort in the Mewar region after chittorgarh. The fort was built in the 15h century AD by Rana Kumbha.With a wall over 36 kms long, the fort has the 2nd largest wall in the world after the great wall of China.
